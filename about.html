About Us

Kids YesIntelligent is dedicated to helping parents and educators keep children engaged, active, and away from excessive screen time. Our mission is to provide high-quality, printable activity kits and worksheets that foster creativity, learning, and fun for kids of all ages.

Our Story

Founded by passionate educators and parents, Kids YesIntelligent was born out of the need for meaningful, screen-free activities for children. We believe that every child deserves the opportunity to learn and grow in a healthy, interactive environment. Our resources are designed to support holistic development—intellectually, emotionally, and socially.

What We Offer

  • 12,000+ printable worksheets and activity kits
  • Fun and educational content for all age groups
  • Resources for parents, teachers, and caregivers
  • Lifetime access to all materials
  • Regular updates and new activities

Our Commitment

We are committed to providing safe, high-quality, and engaging resources for children. Your child’s growth and happiness are at the heart of everything we do. We welcome feedback and suggestions to help us improve and serve you better.
